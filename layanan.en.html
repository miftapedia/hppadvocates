Industrial Relations

Practical & Strategic Industrial Relations Assistance

Drafting of Company Regulations (PP), Collective Labor Agreements (PKB), handling of terminations, and resolution of industrial relations disputes.

  • Drafting, review, and implementation of Company Regulations (PP) & Collective Labor Agreements (PKB)
  • Assistance at the Industrial Relations Court (PHI), mediation, and alternative dispute resolution
  • Termination management, severance negotiations, and fair settlement packages
  • Labor compliance audit and preparation of HR documents (contracts, policies, SOPs)
  • Bipartite negotiations, remuneration policies, and flexible work arrangements

In addition to case resolution, we provide retainer packages to continuously support HR teams: compliance training, periodic policy reviews, and strategic advice when planning restructuring.
