Corporate Experience for Your Business Security

In a complex and ever-changing world of business and law, having a legal advisor who is not only technically proficient but also understands the Client's business objectives is a must. Hendra Pratama & Partners Law Office is here to meet that need.

We were founded on the belief that law should be a tool to facilitate progress and protect achievements. Our focus is on building long-term partnerships, where we invest in truly understanding the business landscape and the legal challenges our Clients face.

With a commitment to uncompromising integrity, excellence in service and a strategic approach to solutions, we are dedicated to being an extension of your in-house legal team with the goal of: Your success is our success.

With a solid understanding of legal science and based on the enforcement of professional ethics, we protect and maximize the legal position and interests of our Clients. We invite you to make us your trusted partner in navigating your business and legal journey.
